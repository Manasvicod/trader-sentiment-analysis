Methodology:
We analyzed Hyperliquid trader activity and merged it with Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear & Greed index). Key behavioral metrics such as trade frequency, leverage usage, position size, and daily PnL were analyzed to understand how sentiment influences trading behavior.

Key Findings:

Trading activity increases significantly during Fear periods due to higher volatility.

Traders increase leverage and trade size during Greed periods.

High-frequency traders demonstrate more stable performance.

Strategy Recommendations:

Reduce leverage during Fear periods to control downside risk.

Apply momentum trading strategies during Greed periods.
